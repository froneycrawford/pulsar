Timing of PALFA Millisecond Pulsars

The details of the timing results and discussion of these four millisecond pulsars are presented in "Four Highly Dispersed Millisecond Pulsars Discovered in the Arecibo PALFA Galactic Plane Survey" by F. Crawford et al., Astrophysical Journal, 757, 90 (2012).

The ephemeris files are in TEMPO format.

All profiles are at a frequency of 1.4 GHz.
